The Chennai Super Kings, also known as CSK, are a professional Twenty20 cricket team based in Chennai, Tamil Nadu, that competes in the Indian Premier League. The team was one of the eight debut franchises when the league was established in 2008.
Captain: MS Dhoni
Owner: Chennai Super Kings Cricket Ltd
Arena/Stadium: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium
Coach: Stephen Fleming (Head coach)
Training ground: M. A. Chidambaram Stadium
Location: Chennai
CLT20 wins: 2 (2010, 2014)
Founded: 24 January 2008; 17 years ago
IPL wins: 5 (2010, 2011, 2018, 2021, 2023)
League: Indian Premier League
Manager: Russell Radhakrishnan
